Insurance Coverage Guide

Third Party Liability Insurance

Essential coverage for accidents involving third parties:

  • Third party death or injury compensation
  • Property damage protection
  • Legal liability coverage
  • Emergency roadside assistance

Recommended for: Budget-conscious drivers seeking basic legal protection

Comprehensive Motor Insurance

Complete protection for your vehicle and third parties:

  • All Third Party Liability benefits
  • Vehicle damage coverage
  • Natural disaster protection
  • Theft protection
  • Personal accident coverage
  • 24/7 roadside assistance

Recommended for: New vehicles and drivers wanting complete peace of mind
