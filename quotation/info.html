Frequently Asked Questions

What types of coverage do you offer?
We offer comprehensive, collision, liability, and personal injury protection coverage, along with additional options tailored to your needs.
How can I file a claim?
You can file a claim 24/7 through our online portal, mobile app, or by calling our dedicated claims hotline.
What factors affect my insurance premium?
Factors include your driving history, vehicle type, age, location, and annual mileage.
Do you offer multi-car discounts?
Yes, we offer significant discounts for insuring multiple vehicles under the same policy.
What is the claims process?
Our claims process is simple: report the incident, provide documentation, and we'll guide you through the rest.
Are there any safe driver discounts?
Yes, we reward safe drivers with discounts up to 20% on their premiums.
What is the minimum coverage required?
Minimum coverage varies by state. Our agents can help determine your state's requirements.
How can I pay my premium?
We offer multiple payment options including online, automatic payments, and monthly installments.
Do you cover rental cars?
Yes, we offer rental car coverage as part of our comprehensive insurance packages.
What is your cancellation policy?
You can cancel your policy at any time. Any unused premium will be refunded.
