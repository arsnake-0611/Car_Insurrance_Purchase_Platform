Vehicle Insurance Application

How to Use This Website

Before You Start

  • Review the available coverage plans in detail
  • Have your vehicle information ready
  • Select your preferred coverage plan
  • Click "Apply Now" to start your application

Application Process

  1. Fill in Vehicle Information:
    • Select vehicle brand and model
    • Enter manufacturing year (YYYY format)
    • Choose body type
    • Provide vehicle specifications
  2. Enter Personal Information:
    • Provide contact details
    • Enter driving experience
    • Confirm coverage plan
  3. Review and Submit:
    • Verify all information
    • Submit application
    • Save reference number

After Submission

  • Track application status in dashboard
  • Search using reference number
  • View or delete applications

Vehicle Information

Enter 4 digits for year (1900-2023)
Enter seating capacity (2-9)
Enter CC value (500-8000)

Personal Information

Enter your 8-digit phone number (e.g., 12345678)

Insurance Coverage Options

Third Party Liability Insurance

Basic protection that provides compensation in case of accidents where you are liable:

  • Third party death compensation
  • Third party bodily injury coverage
  • Property damage protection

Comprehensive Motor Insurance

Enhanced protection including:

  • All Third Party Liability coverage
  • Protection against traffic accidents
  • Flood and fire damage coverage
  • Theft protection
  • Medical and personal accident coverage
  • Death and bodily injury compensation
